What is SAGA?

SAGA is an organization under the course of Bachelors of Science in Entertainment and Multimedia Computing. This organization is designed to help improve students with their skills, knowledge, confidence and become ready to the industry.

Mission

To deliver knowledge and service that empower the the EMC program and it's students.

Vission

To deliver knowledge and service that empower the the EMC program and it's students.

Objective

1. Assist Students in Skill development
2. Offer Experience that will improve their creativity and work.
3. To make other EMC students be helpful to one another even outside the organization by sharing the knowledge they have obtained in the organization.
